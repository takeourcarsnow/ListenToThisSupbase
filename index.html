Help & Shortcuts
ascii.fm is a minimal music threads app. Share music, tag posts, create playlists, and discuss—all in a fast, keyboard-friendly interface.
Works with YouTube, Spotify, Bandcamp, SoundCloud, and direct audio links.
Keyboard Shortcuts
• / focus search
• n focus composer
• j / k queue next / prev
• l like highlighted
• o toggle player on highlighted
• ? toggle this help
Mouse & Touch Tips
• Click tag to filter posts
• Click play all to queue all visible posts
• Use edit or delete on your posts
• Click share to copy or share a post link
Pro tip: Use tags to organize music by genre, mood, or year. Try #chill, #2020, #jazz, etc.
Data is stored locally or synced with Supabase if enabled.
